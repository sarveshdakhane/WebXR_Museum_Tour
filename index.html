Welcome to Siegerlandmuseum AR Experience

Immerse yourself in the rich history of Siegerland using Augmented Reality.

Click Start AR to begin your virtual museum tour.

AR Experience Requirements

This AR experience is supported only on the latest version of Chrome on Android devices. Please ensure the following:

  • Your browser supports WebXR
  • The dom-overlay and image-tracking features are enabled.
  • Camera permissions are granted for augmented reality.
  • Headphone use is recommended for an immersive spatial sound experience.

Steps to Start

  1. Ensure you have the latest version of Chrome installed on your Android device.
  2. Open Chrome and go to chrome://flags.
  3. In the flags search bar, find and enable WebXR Incubations.
  4. Restart Chrome after enabling WebXR Incubations.
  5. Click on Start AR to begin your virtual tour.
  6. Grant camera access when prompted after starting the AR experience.
